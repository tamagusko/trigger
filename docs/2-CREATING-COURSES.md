# Creating Your First Course

Step-by-step guide to create an online course.

## Step 1: Login

1. Open http://studio.local.overhang.io in your browser
2. Click "Sign In"
3. Enter your email and password
4. Click "Sign In"

## Step 2: Create a New Course

1. Click the blue button **"+ New Course"**

2. Fill in the form:
   - **Course Name:** Give your course a title
     Example: "Introduction to Cycling Safety"

   - **Organization:** Your organization name
     Example: "TRIGGER"

   - **Course Number:** A short code
     Example: "CS101"

   - **Course Run:** Year or term
     Example: "2024"

3. Click **"Create"**

## Step 3: Add Course Content

### Understanding Course Structure

Think of your course like a book:
- **Sections** = Chapters (Week 1, Week 2, etc.)
- **Subsections** = Lessons within chapters
- **Units** = Pages within lessons
- **Components** = Actual content (videos, text, quizzes)

### Add a Section (Week or Module)

1. Click **"+ New Section"**
2. Type a name: "Week 1: Introduction"
3. Press Enter

### Add a Subsection (Lesson)

1. Click **"+ New Subsection"** under your section
2. Type a name: "Lesson 1: Road Safety Basics"
3. Press Enter

### Add a Unit (Content Page)

1. Click **"+ New Unit"** under your subsection
2. Type a name: "Welcome Video"
3. Press Enter

## Step 4: Add Content to Your Unit

You can add different types of content:

### Add a Video

1. Click the **"Video"** button
2. Click **"Edit"**
3. In the "Default Video URL" field, paste a YouTube link
4. Click **"Save"**

### Add Text

1. Click the **"Text"** or **"HTML"** button
2. Click **"Edit"**
3. Type or paste your content
4. Use the toolbar to format (bold, italic, lists, etc.)
5. Click **"Save"**

### Add a Quiz Question

1. Click the **"Problem"** button
2. Select **"Multiple Choice"**
3. Click **"Edit"**
4. Write your question
5. Add answer choices
6. Mark the correct answer with (x)
7. Click **"Save"**

Example:
```
>>Which is safest for cycling?<<

( ) No helmet
(x) Wearing a helmet
( ) Cycling at night without lights
```

## Step 5: Publish Your Content

1. Click the green **"Publish"** button (top right)
2. Your content is now visible to students!

## Step 6: Set Course Dates

1. Click **"Settings"** (top menu)
2. Click **"Schedule & Details"**
3. Set:
   - **Course Start Date:** When students can access the course
   - **Course End Date:** When the course closes
4. Scroll down and click **"Save Changes"**

---

## Adding More Content

### To Add More Units
Just click **"+ New Unit"** and repeat Step 4

### To Add More Lessons
Click **"+ New Subsection"** and add units inside

### To Add More Weeks
Click **"+ New Section"** at the top level

---

## Viewing as a Student

1. Click **"View Live"** (top right corner)
2. This shows how students see your course
3. Test everything before releasing to students!

---

## Tips

- **Keep videos short** - 5-10 minutes is best
- **Add quizzes** - Help students check their understanding
- **Use clear names** - Make it easy to navigate
- **Test everything** - View as student before publishing
- **Save often** - Click Save after each edit
